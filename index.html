About

Let's get to change our lives!

  • 2009-2011

    Our Humble Beginnings

    In the early years, fueled by passion and a vision for productivity, our team laid the foundation for the to-do list application. We aimed to create a tool that simplifies task management and enhances efficiency.

  • March 2011

    An Agency is Born

    March 2011 marked a pivotal moment as our to-do list application evolved into a full-fledged agency. With a growing user base, we expanded our team and resources, solidifying our commitment to providing an exceptional task management experience.

  • December 2015

    Transition to Full Service

    In December 2015, recognizing the diverse needs of our users, we transitioned our to-do list application into a comprehensive full-service platform. This transition allowed us to offer a more feature-rich experience, empowering users to organize their tasks with greater flexibility and efficiency.

  • July 2020

    Phase Two Expansion

    Embarking on phase two expansion in July 2020, we introduced innovative features and improvements to elevate the to-do list app. This expansion aimed to address user feedback and embrace emerging technologies, ensuring our application remains at the forefront of task management solutions.
